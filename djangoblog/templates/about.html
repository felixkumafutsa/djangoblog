About Us

Girl Engineer is a Malawi-based registered non-profit organization that is dedicated to creating opportunities that inspire, educate, and empower young girls to pursue STEM fields. Our organization firmly believes in the immense potential of young girls to achieve greatness in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics fields, and we tirelessly work to create opportunities that inspire, educate, and empower them.

Our primary focus is on designing and delivering hands-on STEM outreach workshops to primary schools. We provide interactive learning experiences to increase STEM awareness and encourage girls to explore the possibilities of these exciting fields. We are convinced that a solid foundation in STEM education will not only benefit individual girls but also contribute to the development of Malawi's economy and society as a whole.

Besides our outreach workshops, we also organize STEM tours and expose young girls to STEM role models. Through these activities, we hope to create a community of supportive and engaged individuals who are committed to closing the gender gap in STEM fields of study and careers. We believe that exposing young girls to real-world applications of STEM subjects and showing them real job opportunities will help them make informed decisions about their future education and career choices.

We also engage in community awareness campaigns that aim to challenge the gender stereotypes that hinder girls and women from pursuing STEM fields. Through these campaigns, we educate parents and guardians on how they can encourage their daughters to pursue STEM fields just as much as their sons and how they can support their daughters' STEM education opportunities.

At Girl Engineer, we are committed to empowering young girls to pursue their dreams in STEM fields. We believe that every girl deserves an equal opportunity to excel in their education, regardless of their financial background. As such, we have established a STEM Sponsorship program that offers various forms of support to needy girls who have demonstrated interest in STEM careers.

All of our five programs, namely STEM workshops, STEM role models, STEM tours, STEM sponsorship, and STEM awareness, are interconnected. Through STEM workshops, we spark young girls' interest and curiosity in STEM fields of study and careers. Then we expose them to STEM role models so that they see that other women and girls are succeeding in STEM. Later, through STEM tours, we show them real-world applications of STEM subjects and expose them to real job opportunities. Through our STEM Sponsorship program, we go further to support financially challenged girls who have demonstrated interest in STEM and are at risk of dropping out of school due to financial constraints to stay in school. Finally, through our STEM awareness campaigns, we educate parents and guardians on how they can encourage their daughters to pursue STEM fields just as much as their sons and how they can support their daughters' STEM education opportunities.

Our mission at Girl Engineer is to create opportunities that inspire, educate, and empower young girls to pursue STEM fields of study and careers. We firmly believe that every girl has the potential to make a meaningful contribution to the world of STEM and that they deserve the opportunity to explore these fields in a supportive and empowering environment.

Our vision is to close the gender gap in STEM fields of study and careers. We strive to create a future where girls have equal access to STEM education, resources, and opportunities, and where they can fulfill their potential and achieve their dreams.

We are committed to making a positive difference in the lives of young girls in Malawi, and we invite you to join us on this journey. Together, we can inspire the next generation of female leaders in STEM and create a more equitable and prosperous world for all..
