About Girl Engineer

Girl Engineer is a Malawi-based registered non-profit organization that is dedicated to creating opportunities that inspire, educate, and empower young girls to pursue STEM fields. Our organization firmly believes in the immense potential of young girls to achieve greatness in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics fields, and we tirelessly work to create opportunities that inspire, educate, and empower them.

Benefits of Partnership

Our partners will take advantage of the partnership to publicize their brand and gain potential customers' trust and affection. By partnering with us, your organization will actively contribute to reducing the gender gap in STEM and make a positive impact on the lives of young girls.

Partnership Opportunities

We offer a range of partnership opportunities that allow organizations to actively participate in our mission of inspiring, educating, and empowering young girls in STEM fields. By partnering with us, you can make a meaningful impact on the lives of girls and contribute to a more diverse and inclusive future in STEM.

STEM Workshops

Our STEM workshops are an integral part of our outreach efforts. As a partner, you can support these workshops by making recurring donations or providing necessary resources. Your contribution will help us deliver engaging and hands-on STEM activities to girls in primary schools. These workshops not only provide valuable learning experiences but also foster curiosity, critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a passion for STEM subjects.

STEM Tours

By organizing STEM tours, partners have the opportunity to showcase their companies or organizations and inspire young girls through real-world applications of STEM. During these tours, girls will visit your facilities, interact with professionals in STEM fields, and gain insights into various career paths. Your organization's support in arranging these tours and covering the associated expenses will have a lasting impact on the girls, helping them connect classroom learning with practical experiences.

STEM Sponsorship

Our STEM Sponsorship program aims to remove financial barriers that hinder girls' access to STEM education. Through this program, partners can make a significant difference in the lives of deserving girls by providing financial support. Your sponsorship can cover school fees, textbooks, educational materials, and other essential resources. By enabling girls from disadvantaged backgrounds to pursue their education in STEM, you are helping them unlock their potential and create a better future for themselves and their communities.

STEM Awareness

The STEM awareness campaign is a crucial initiative to create a more inclusive environment for girls in STEM. As a partner, you can join us in raising awareness about the importance of gender equality in STEM and highlighting the contributions of women in these fields. Together, we can organize events, workshops, seminars, and panel discussions that promote dialogue and challenge stereotypes. By spreading the message of diversity and inclusivity, we can inspire young girls to pursue STEM and encourage parents, educators, and the wider community to support their aspirations.

STEM Role Models

Representation matters. Through our STEM Role Models program, partners have the opportunity to provide female professionals who can serve as mentors and role models for young girls. Your organization can contribute by arranging guest speaker sessions, where successful women in STEM share their experiences, challenges, and achievements. By sharing their stories and insights, these role models can inspire and empower girls, showing them that they too can thrive in STEM careers.

How to Become a Partner

If you are interested in partnering with us or would like to discuss partnership opportunities in further detail, please contact us at girlengineermw@gmail.com. We look forward to hearing from you and working together to create a brighter future for young girls in STEM.

Thank you for your interest in partnering with Girl Engineer!
